What to Expect from Therapy

Starting therapy is a brave step towards well-being. Here’s what to keep in mind:

  • ✓
    Confidentiality is Key: Everything you share is kept private and secure. It's a safe space.
  • ✓
    Be Open and Honest: The more open you are, the more your therapist can help you. There is no judgment.
  • ✓
    It's a Process: Healing and growth take time. Be patient and compassionate with yourself.
  • ✓
    Your Therapist is a Guide: They are there to support you, offer new perspectives, and provide tools to help you navigate challenges.
  • ✓
    You Are in Control: You set the pace and decide what you want to talk about. This is your journey.
